About This App

This application uses information from Ferrari to help display the history of the company, its many models, and more. By presenting this information in a organize manner it helps car fanatics learn more about the era’s within the company’s history, a timeline of all the cars and their information, and save the car information. Also the app includes certain information based on many factors such as the year, body, engine, and other specifications.
